WKB近似与量子隧穿

WKB近似[2]

$$ \newcommand{\d}{\mathrm{d}} \newcommand{\i}{\mathrm{i}} $$

薛定谔方程

$$ E\psi(x) = -\cfrac{\hbar^2}{2m} \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2}{\mathrm{d} x^2} \psi(x) + V(x) \psi(x) $$
$$ \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2}{\mathrm{d} x^2} \psi(x) = \cfrac{2m}{\hbar^2} \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack \psi(x) $$

非经典区域

此时 $V(x)\gt E$

设波函数形式为

$$ \psi(x) = \exp \left\lbrack S(x)/\hbar\right\rbrack $$
$$ \psi^\prime(x) = 1/\hbar S^\prime(x) \exp \left\lbrack S(x)/\hbar\right\rbrack $$
$$ \psi^{\prime\prime}(x) = 1/\hbar S^{\prime\prime}(x) \exp \left\lbrack S(x)/\hbar\right\rbrack +1/\hbar^2 \left\lbrack S^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 \exp \left\lbrack S(x)/\hbar\right\rbrack $$

代入薛定谔方程中

$$ \hbar S^{\prime\prime}(x) + \left\lbrack S^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 = 2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack $$

接下来把 $S(x)$ 按 $\hbar$ 的阶展开

$$ S(x) = S_0(x) + \hbar S_1(x) + ... $$

代入得

$$ \hbar S_0^{\prime\prime}(x) + \hbar^2 S_1^{\prime\prime}(x) + \left\lbrack S_0^\prime(x) + \hbar S_1^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 = 2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack $$
$$ \hbar S_0^{\prime\prime}(x) + \hbar^2 S_1^{\prime\prime}(x) + \left\lbrack S_0^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 + \hbar^2 \left\lbrack S_1^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 + 2 \hbar S_0^\prime(x) S_1^\prime(x) = 2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack $$

忽略二阶项，令零阶项和一阶项分别为 $0$ ，

$$ \begin{cases} \left\lbrack S_0^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 = 2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack \\ S_0^{\prime\prime}(x) = -2S_0^\prime(x) S_1^\prime(x) \\ \end{cases} $$

解得

$$ \begin{cases} S_0(x) = \pm\int_{x_0}^x \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack } \,\mathrm{d} x \\ S_1(x) = \int -\cfrac{S_0^{\prime\prime}(x)}{2S_0^\prime(x)} \,\mathrm{d} x = -\frac{1}{2}\ln \lvert S_0^{\prime}(x)\rvert + \ln C \end{cases} $$

其中 $x_0,C$ 和初始条件和波函数归一化有关

定义“动量”

$$ q(x) = \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack } $$

$S_0(x)$ 分别取正负号给出两组解，线性组合后给出通解

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{C_1}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x_0}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{C_2}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x_0}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

经典区域

此时 $V(x)\lt E$

采用计算非经典区域时类似的方法，

设波函数形式为

$$ \psi(x) = \exp \left\lbrack \mathrm{i} S(x)/\hbar\right\rbrack $$

代入薛定谔方程中

$$ \mathrm{i} \hbar S^{\prime\prime}(x) - \left\lbrack S^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 = 2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack $$

把 $S(x)$ 按 $\hbar$ 的阶展开

$$ S(x) = S_0(x) + \hbar S_1(x) + ... $$

代入得

$$ \begin{cases} \left\lbrack S_0^\prime(x)\right\rbrack ^2 = 2m \left\lbrack E - V(x)\right\rbrack \\ \mathrm{i} S_0^{\prime\prime}(x) = 2S_0^\prime(x) S_1^\prime(x) \\ \end{cases} $$

定义“动量”

$$ p(x) = \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack E - V(x)\right\rbrack } $$
$$ \begin{cases} S_0(x) = \pm \int_{x_0}^x p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \\ S_1(x) = \int \mathrm{i}\cfrac{S_0^{\prime\prime}(x)}{2S_0^\prime(x)} \,\mathrm{d} x = \frac{\mathrm{i}}{2}\ln \lvert S_0^{\prime}(x)\rvert - \mathrm{i} \ln C \end{cases} $$

得到通解

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{C_1}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x_0}^x p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{C_2}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x_0}^x p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

Turning point

经典区域和非经典区域的连接区域，此时 $p(x),q(x) \rightarrow 0$ ，两边的近似表达式都不成立。

Turning point附近做泰勒展开，假设势能和坐标线性关系。进一步假设左边是经典区，右边是非经典区，分界点 $x=a$ 。

$$ V(x) - E = F_1 (x - a) $$

薛定谔方程成为

$$ \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2}{\mathrm{d} x^2} \psi(x) = \cfrac{2mF_1}{\hbar^2} (x - a) \psi(x) $$

令

$$ u = \sqrt\lbrack 3\rbrack {\cfrac{2mF_1}{\hbar^2}}(x-a) $$

则

$$ \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2 \psi}{\mathrm{d} u^2} = u \psi $$

此即Airy方程，通解为

$$ \psi = C_1 \mathrm{Ai}(u) + C_2 \mathrm{Bi}(u) $$

Turning point附近波函数系数之间的关系

Airy函数在 $u \to -\infty, u \to +\infty$ 的渐进表达式为[1]

$$ \begin{gathered} \mathrm{Ai}(u) \approx \cfrac{1}{2\sqrt{\pi}u^{1/4}}\exp\left(-\frac{2}{3}u^{3/2}\right) & u \to +\infty \\ \mathrm{Ai}(u) \approx \cfrac{1}{\sqrt{\pi}\lvert u \rvert^{1/4}}\cos\left(\frac{2}{3}\lvert u \rvert^{3/2} - \frac{\pi}{4}\right) & u \to -\infty \\ \mathrm{Bi}(u) \approx \cfrac{1}{\sqrt{\pi}u^{1/4}}\exp\left(\frac{2}{3}u^{3/2}\right) & u \to +\infty \\ \mathrm{Bi}(u) \approx \cfrac{1}{\sqrt{\pi}\lvert u \rvert^{1/4}}\cos\left(\frac{2}{3}\lvert u \rvert^{3/2} + \frac{\pi}{4}\right) & u \to -\infty \\ \\\end{gathered} $$

左边经典区

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{A_1}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{A_2}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

其中

$$ \begin{gathered} p(x) \approx \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack F_1 (a - x)\right\rbrack } \\ = \sqrt{2mF_1\sqrt\lbrack 3\rbrack {\cfrac{\hbar^2}{2mF_1}} \lvert u \rvert} \\ = (2mF_1\hbar)^{1/3} \lvert u \rvert^{1/2} \\\end{gathered} $$

再计算指数里的积分

$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \approx \cfrac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack F_1 (a - x)\right\rbrack } \,\mathrm{d} x \\ = \cfrac{\sqrt{2mF_1}}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a \sqrt{a - x} \,\mathrm{d} x \\ = \cfrac{2\sqrt{2mF_1}}{3\hbar} (a - x)^{3/2} \\ = \frac{2}{3} \lvert u \rvert^{3/2} \\\end{gathered} $$

于是左边经典区：

$$ \psi(x) \approx \cfrac{A_1}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6}\lvert u \rvert^{1/4}} \exp \left( \mathrm{i} \frac{2}{3} \lvert u \rvert^{3/2} \right) + \cfrac{A_2}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6} \lvert u \rvert^{1/4}} \exp \left( -\mathrm{i} \frac{2}{3} \lvert u \rvert^{3/2} \right) $$

根据

$$ \exp(\mathrm{i} x) = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}(1 + \mathrm{i}) \cos(x - \pi/4) + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} (1 - \mathrm{i}) \cos(x + \pi/4) $$

得

$$ \psi(x) \approx \cfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{\sqrt{2}(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6}} \left\{ \left\lbrack A_1(1+\mathrm{i})+A_2(1-\mathrm{i})\right\rbrack \mathrm{Ai}(u) + \left\lbrack A_1(1-\mathrm{i})+A_2(1+\mathrm{i})\right\rbrack \mathrm{Bi}(u) \right\} $$

右边非经典区

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{B_1}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{B_2}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

同理

$$ q(x) \approx (2mF_1\hbar)^{1/3} u^{1/2} $$
$$ \cfrac{1}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \approx \frac{2}{3} u^{3/2} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} \psi(x) \approx \cfrac{B_1}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6} u^{1/4}} \exp \left( \frac{2}{3} u^{3/2} \right) + \cfrac{B_2}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6} u^{1/4}} \exp \left( -\frac{2}{3} u^{3/2} \right) \\ \approx \cfrac{\sqrt{\pi}B_1}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6}} \mathrm{Bi}(u) + \cfrac{2\sqrt{\pi}B_2}{(2mF_1\hbar)^{1/6}} \mathrm{Ai}(u) \\ \\\end{gathered} $$

波函数系数之间的关系

综合上述计算

$$ \begin{cases} A_1(1+\mathrm{i})+A_2(1-\mathrm{i}) = 2\sqrt{2}B_2 \\ A_1(1-\mathrm{i})+A_2(1+\mathrm{i}) = \sqrt{2}B_1 \\ \end{cases} $$

上式的系数关系是左边经典区&右边非经典区的条件下，如果左边是非经典区&右边是经典区，则

$$ \begin{cases} A_2(1+\mathrm{i})+A_1(1-\mathrm{i}) = 2\sqrt{2}B_1 \\ A_2(1-\mathrm{i})+A_1(1+\mathrm{i}) = \sqrt{2}B_2 \\ \end{cases} $$

任意势垒量子隧穿计算

设同种材料（粒子质量不随坐标变化）

设从左到右分别为经典区I，非经典区II，经典区III。非经典区II位于 $x\in (a,b)$

经典区I波函数

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{A_1}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{A_2}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{x}^a p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

非经典区II波函数

$$ \begin{gathered} \psi(x) = \cfrac{B_1}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{B_2}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack \\ = \cfrac{B_1\exp(D)}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x}^b q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{B_2\exp(-D)}{\sqrt{q(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack \frac{1}{\hbar} \int_{x}^b q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack \\ \\\end{gathered} $$

其中

$$ D = \cfrac{1}{\hbar} \int_a^b q(x) \,\mathrm{d} x $$

经典区III波函数

$$ \psi(x) = \cfrac{C_1}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack +\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack + \cfrac{C_2}{\sqrt{p(x)}} \exp \left\lbrack -\frac{\mathrm{i}}{\hbar} \int_{a}^x p(x) \,\mathrm{d} x \right\rbrack $$

利用上节结论，

$$ \begin{cases} A_1(1+\mathrm{i})+A_2(1-\mathrm{i}) = 2\sqrt{2}B_2 \\ A_1(1-\mathrm{i})+A_2(1+\mathrm{i}) = \sqrt{2}B_1 \\ C_2(1+\mathrm{i})+C_1(1-\mathrm{i}) = 2\sqrt{2}B_1\exp(D) \\ C_2(1-\mathrm{i})+C_1(1+\mathrm{i}) = \sqrt{2}B_2\exp(-D) \\ \end{cases} $$

不存在从右侧入射波， $C_2=0$

$$ \begin{cases} A_1(1+\mathrm{i})+A_2(1-\mathrm{i}) = 2(1+\mathrm{i})\exp(D)C_1 \\ A_1(1-\mathrm{i})+A_2(1+\mathrm{i}) = \cfrac{1-\mathrm{i}}{2}\exp(-D)C_1 \\ \end{cases} $$

解得

$$ \cfrac{C_1}{A_1} = \cfrac{4}{4\exp(D) - \exp(-D)} \approx \exp(-D) $$

于是传输系数

这里计算的是传输系数，即出射流比入射流，因此需要概率乘上速度 $\propto p(x)\lvert \psi(x) \rvert^2$

$$ T(E) = \cfrac{\lvert C_1 \rvert^2}{\lvert A_1 \rvert^2} \approx \exp \left( -\cfrac{2}{\hbar} \int_a^b \sqrt{2m \left\lbrack V(x) - E\right\rbrack } \,\mathrm{d} x \right) $$
