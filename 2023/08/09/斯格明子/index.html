斯格明子

设二维磁性材料，归一化的磁化场分布 $n=n(x,y)$

Winding number

$$ N = \cfrac{1}{4\pi} \int n \cdot \left( \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial x} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial y} \right) \, \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y $$

二维磁化场的球极映射

假设极限

$$ \lim_{(x,y) \to \infty} n $$

存在（或者说磁矩在无穷远处趋于定值），则可以做球极映射：

$$ \begin{cases} \phi = \arctan \cfrac{y}{x} \\ \theta = 2 \arctan \cfrac{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}{2} \\ \end{cases} $$

反变换

$$ \begin{cases} x = 2 \tan \cfrac{\theta}{2} \cos \phi \\ y = 2 \tan \cfrac{\theta}{2} \sin \phi \\ \end{cases} $$

可以证明平面上的winding number可以对应到球面上

$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial x} = \cfrac{-y}{x^2+y^2}\cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} + \cfrac{1}{1+\cfrac{x^2+y^2}{4}} \cfrac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \\ \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial y} = \cfrac{x}{x^2+y^2}\cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} + \cfrac{1}{1+\cfrac{x^2+y^2}{4}} \cfrac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \\\end{gathered} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial x} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial y} = \cfrac{1}{1+\cfrac{x^2+y^2}{4}} \cfrac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \\ = \cfrac{1}{2 \sec^2 \cfrac{\theta}{2} \tan \cfrac{\theta}{2}} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \\\end{gathered} $$

计算测度

$$ \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = \begin{vmatrix} \cfrac{\partial x}{\partial \theta} & \cfrac{\partial x}{\partial \phi} \\ \cfrac{\partial y}{\partial \theta} & \cfrac{\partial y}{\partial \phi} \\ \end{vmatrix} \mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}\phi = \begin{vmatrix} \sec^2 \theta \cos \phi & -2\tan \cfrac{\theta}{2} \sin \phi \\ \sec^2 \theta \sin \phi & 2\tan \cfrac{\theta}{2} \cos \phi \\ \end{vmatrix} \mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}\phi = 2 \sec^2 \cfrac{\theta}{2} \tan \cfrac{\theta}{2} \mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}\phi $$

于是

$$ \begin{gathered} N = \cfrac{1}{4\pi} \int n \cdot \left( \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial x} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial y} \right) \, \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y \\ = \cfrac{1}{4\pi} \int n \cdot \left( \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \right) \, \mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}\phi \\\end{gathered} $$

可见换到球面后winding number形式不变。

拓扑分类

设

$$ n=(\sin\theta_n\cos\phi_n, \sin\theta_n\sin\phi_n, \cos\theta_n) $$

即用球面坐标表示 $n$ 。

$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \\ = ( \cos\theta_n\cos\phi_n \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} -\sin\theta_n\sin\phi_n \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta}, \cos\theta_n\sin\phi_n \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} +\sin\theta_n\cos\phi_n \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta}, -\sin\theta_n \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} ) \\ = ( \cos\theta_n\cos\phi_n, \cos\theta_n\sin\phi_n, -\sin\theta_n ) \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} +( -\sin\theta_n\sin\phi_n, +\sin\theta_n\cos\phi_n, 0 ) \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta} \\\end{gathered} $$

定义

$$ \begin{gathered} n_1= ( \cos\theta_n\cos\phi_n, \cos\theta_n\sin\phi_n, -\sin\theta_n ) \\ n_2= ( -\sin\theta_n\sin\phi_n, \sin\theta_n\cos\phi_n, 0 ) \\\end{gathered} $$

可以验证 $n_1,n_2,n$ 互相垂直。

则

$$ \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} = n_1 \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} + n_2 \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta} $$

同理有

$$ \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} = n_1 \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \phi} + n_2 \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \phi} $$

则

$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \\ = n_1 \times n_2 \left( \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \phi} - \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \phi} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta} \right) \\ = n \sin \theta_n \left( \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \phi} - \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \phi} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta} \right) \\\end{gathered} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} n \cdot \left(\cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \right) \\ = \sin \theta_n \left( \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \theta} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \phi} - \cfrac{\partial \theta_n}{\partial \phi} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \theta} \right) \\\end{gathered} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} N = \cfrac{1}{4\pi} \int n \cdot \left( \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \theta} \times \cfrac{\partial n}{\partial \phi} \right) \, \mathrm{d}\theta \mathrm{d}\phi \\ = -\cfrac{1}{4\pi} \int_0^\pi \cfrac{\partial \cos \theta_n}{\partial \theta} \mathrm{d} \theta \int_0^{2\pi} \cfrac{\partial \phi_n}{\partial \phi} \mathrm{d}\phi \\ = -\cfrac{1}{4\pi} \left(\cos \theta_n \vert_{\theta=\pi} - \cos \theta_n \vert_{\theta=0} \right) \left(\phi_n \vert_{\phi=2\pi} - \phi_n \vert_{\phi=0} \right) \\\end{gathered} $$

假设无穷远处磁矩向上，原点处向下。假设 $\phi$ 绕一圈后，磁矩绕 $m$ 圈（即 $\phi_n$ 绕 $m$ 圈），则

$$ N=m $$

此即winding number的意义：无穷远处磁矩向上，原点处向下时， $\phi$ 绕一圈后，磁矩绕 $N$ 圈。

假设 $N$ 非 $0$ ，此时不能通过连续变换到磁矩为常数的情形（因为磁矩为常数时 $N=0$ ），此即拓扑保护。
