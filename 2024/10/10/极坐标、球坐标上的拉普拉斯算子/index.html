极坐标、球坐标上的拉普拉斯算子

拉普拉斯算子定义：

$$ \Delta = \nabla^2 = \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial^2 x} + \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial^2 y} + \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial^2 z} $$

一般方法

本质上是换元，将对直角坐标 $x_i$ 对导数化为对极坐标/球坐标 $\theta_j$ 的导数。定义一个矩阵

$$ a_{ij} = \cfrac{\partial \theta_i}{\partial x_j} $$

那么有

$$ \cfrac{\partial}{\partial x_i}=\sum_j a_{ij}\cfrac{\partial}{\partial \theta_j} $$

接下来计算拉普拉斯算子

$$ \sum_i \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial x_i^2}= \sum_{ij} \cfrac{\partial a_{ij}}{\partial x_i}\cfrac{\partial}{\partial \theta_j} +\sum_{ijk} a_{ij}a_{ik}\cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \theta_j \partial \theta_k} $$

简单的写，用矩阵的语言表达：

$$ \nabla^2 = (\nabla_x A) \nabla_\theta + \nabla_\theta (A^T A) \nabla_\theta $$

其中第一项的nabla算子作用到右边 $A$ 上，而第二项应该理解为nabla算子不作用在右边 $A^TA$ 上。

极坐标

极坐标定义：

$$ \begin{cases} x = \rho \cos \theta \\ y = \rho \sin \theta \\ \end{cases} $$
$$ \begin{cases} \rho = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \\ \theta = \arctan \cfrac{y}{x} \\ \end{cases} $$

求矩阵 $A$

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \partial_x \\ \partial_y \\ \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \cos \theta & -\cfrac{\sin\theta}{\rho} \\ \sin \theta & +\cfrac{\cos\theta}{\rho} \\ \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \partial_\rho \\ \partial_\theta \\ \end{pmatrix} $$

计算 $A^TA$

$$ A^TA = \begin{pmatrix} \cos \theta & \sin \theta \\ -\cfrac{\sin\theta}{\rho} & +\cfrac{\cos\theta}{\rho} \\ \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \cos \theta & -\cfrac{\sin\theta}{\rho} \\ \sin \theta & +\cfrac{\cos\theta}{\rho} \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & \cfrac{1}{\rho^2} \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ $$

计算 $\nabla_xA$

$$ \nabla_x A = \begin{pmatrix} \cfrac{1}{\rho} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ $$
$$ \begin{gathered} \partial_x \cfrac{x}{\rho} = \cfrac{\rho-x^2/\rho}{\rho^2} \\ =\cfrac{\rho^2-x^2}{\rho^3} \\\end{gathered} $$

于是

$$ \nabla^2 = \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \rho^2} + \cfrac{1}{\rho} \cfrac{\partial}{\partial \rho} + \cfrac{1}{\rho^2} \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \theta^2} $$

球坐标

球坐标定义：

$$ \begin{cases} x=r\sin\theta\cos\phi \\ y=r\sin\theta\sin\phi \\ z=r\cos\theta \\ \end{cases} $$
$$ \begin{cases} r = \sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2} \\ \theta = \arccos \cfrac{z}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}} \\ \phi = \arctan \cfrac{y}{x} \\ \end{cases} $$

求矩阵 $A$

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \partial_x \\ \partial_y \\ \partial_z \\ \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \sin\theta\cos\phi & \cfrac{\cos \theta \cos \phi}{r} &- \cfrac{\sin\phi}{r\sin\theta} \\ \sin\theta\sin\phi & \cfrac{\cos \theta \sin \phi}{r} & \cfrac{\cos\phi}{r\sin\theta} \\ \cos\theta &- \cfrac{\sin \theta}{r} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \partial_r \\ \partial_\theta \\ \partial_\phi \\ \end{pmatrix} $$

矩阵 $A$ ，替换成直角坐标的变量

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \cfrac{x}{r} & \cfrac{xz}{r^2\rho} &- \cfrac{y}{\rho^2}\\ \cfrac{y}{r} & \cfrac{yz}{r^2\rho} & \cfrac{x}{\rho^2} \\ \cfrac{z}{r} &- \cfrac{\rho}{r^2} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} $$

计算 $A^TA$

$$ \begin{gathered} \begin{pmatrix} \sin\theta\cos\phi &\sin\theta\sin\phi &\cos\theta \\

\cfrac{\cos \theta \cos \phi}{r}
& \cfrac{\cos \theta \sin \phi}{r}
&- \cfrac{\sin \theta}{r} \

  • \cfrac{\sin\phi}{r\sin\theta}
    & \cfrac{\cos\phi}{r\sin\theta}
    & 0 \
    \end{pmatrix}
    \begin{pmatrix}
    \sin\theta\cos\phi
    & \cfrac{\cos \theta \cos \phi}{r}
    &- \cfrac{\sin\phi}{r\sin\theta} \
    \sin\theta\sin\phi
    & \cfrac{\cos \theta \sin \phi}{r}
    & \cfrac{\cos\phi}{r\sin\theta} \
    \cos\theta
    &- \cfrac{\sin \theta}{r}
    & 0 \
    \end{pmatrix} \

    \begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0 & 0 \
    0 & \cfrac{1}{r^2} & 0 \
    0 & 0 & \cfrac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta} \
    \end{pmatrix}
    \end{gathered}
    $$

计算 $\nabla_xA$

$$ \nabla_x A = \begin{pmatrix} \cfrac{2}{r} & \cfrac{\cos \theta}{r^2\sin\theta} & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ $$

于是

$$ \nabla^2 = \cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial r^2}+\cfrac{2}{r}\cfrac{\partial}{\partial r} +\cfrac{1}{r^2}\cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \theta^2} +\cfrac{\cos \theta}{r^2\sin\theta}\cfrac{\partial}{\partial \theta} +\cfrac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta}\cfrac{\partial^2}{\partial \phi^2} $$
