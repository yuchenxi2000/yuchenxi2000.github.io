Numerov method

$$ \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2y}{\mathrm{d}x^2}=-gy+s \tag{1} $$
$$ \left(1+\cfrac{h^2}{12}g_{n+1}\right)y_{n+1}-2\left(1-\cfrac{5h^2}{12}g_{n}\right)y_{n}+\left(1+\cfrac{h^2}{12}g_{n-1}\right)y_{n-1} = \cfrac{h^2}{12}(s_{n+1}+10s_n+s_{n-1}) \tag{2} $$

推导：

$$ \begin{gathered} y_{n+1}=y_n+hy_n'+\cfrac{h^2}{2}y_n''+\cfrac{h^3}{6}y_n'''+\cfrac{h^4}{24}y_n^{(4)}+o(h^4) \\ y_{n-1}=y_n-hy_n'+\cfrac{h^2}{2}y_n''-\cfrac{h^3}{6}y_n'''+\cfrac{h^4}{24}y_n^{(4)}+o(h^4) \\\end{gathered} \tag{3} $$
$$ y_{n+1}+y_{n-1}=2y_n+h^2y_n''+\cfrac{h^4}{24}y_n^{(4)}+o(h^4) \tag{4} $$
$$ \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2y}{\mathrm{d}x^2}=\cfrac{y_{n+1}-2y_n+y_{n-1}}{h^2}-\cfrac{h^2}{12}\cfrac{\mathrm{d}^4y}{\mathrm{d}x^4}+o(h^4) \tag{5} $$

根据原方程有

$$ \begin{gathered} \cfrac{\mathrm{d}^4y}{\mathrm{d}x^4}=\cfrac{\mathrm{d}^2(s-gy)}{\mathrm{d}x^2} \\ = \cfrac{s_{n+1}-2s_n+s_{n-1}}{h^2}-\cfrac{g_{n+1}y_{n+1}-2g_ny_n+g_{n-1}y_{n-1}}{h^2}+o(h^2) \\\end{gathered} \tag{6} $$

所以

$$ s_n-g_ny_n=\cfrac{y_{n+1}-2y_n+y_{n-1}}{h^2}-\cfrac{s_{n+1}-2s_n+s_{n-1}}{12}+\cfrac{g_{n+1}y_{n+1}-2g_ny_n+g_{n-1}y_{n-1}}{12}+o(h^4) \tag{7} $$
$$ \cfrac{h^2}{12}(s_{n+1}+10s_n+s_{n-1})=y_{n+1}-2y_n+y_{n-1}+\cfrac{h^2}{12}(g_{n+1}y_{n+1}+10g_ny_n+g_{n-1}y_{n-1})+o(h^6) \tag{8} $$

整理后即Numerov方法。

非均匀格点

对于非均匀格点，做换元使得 $u$ 为均匀格点

$$ x=x(u) \tag{9} $$

计算换元后的方程

$$ y_x = \cfrac{y_u}{x_u} \tag{10} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} y_{xx} = \cfrac{(y_x)_u}{x_u} \\ = \cfrac{y_{uu}x_u-y_ux_{uu}}{x_u^3} \\\end{gathered} \tag{11} $$
$$ y_{uu}-\cfrac{x_{uu}}{x_u}y_u=-x_u^2gy+x_u^2s \tag{12} $$

Numerov算法的要求不能出现一阶导数，因此设法消去一阶导数

因为

$$ (vy)_{uu} = v y_{uu} + 2 v_uy_u + v_{uu}y \tag{13} $$

因此令

$$ \cfrac{2v_u}{v} = -\cfrac{x_{uu}}{x_u} \tag{14} $$

即可消去一阶导数。

解上面这个微分方程

$$ 2(\ln v)_u = -(\ln x_u)_u \tag{15} $$

积分常数可以任取，我们选择

$$ v=(x_u)^{-1/2} \tag{16} $$

于是

$$ \begin{gathered} vy_{uu}+2v_uy_u=-(x_u)^{3/2}gy+(x_u)^{3/2}s \\ (vy)_{uu}=v_{uu}y-(x_u)^{3/2}gy+(x_u)^{3/2}s \\ (vy)_{uu}=-g_{\text{eff}}y+s_{\text{eff}} \\ \\\end{gathered} \tag{17} $$

该方程有类似形式，其中

$$ \begin{gathered} g_{\text{eff}} = (x_u)^2g-\cfrac{v_{uu}}{v} \\ s_{\text{eff}} = (x_u)^{3/2}s \\\end{gathered} \tag{18} $$
