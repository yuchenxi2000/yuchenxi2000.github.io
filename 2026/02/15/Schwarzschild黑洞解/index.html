Schwarzschild黑洞解

符号约定与一些定义

闵可夫斯基度规符号约定：

$$ \eta_{\mu\nu} = (-1, +1, +1, +1) \tag{1} $$

矢量平移、平行移动与克里斯托弗符号：

$$ D_{k}A_j=\partial_kA_j-\Gamma^i_{jk}A_i \tag{2} $$
$$ D_k A^j = \partial_k A^j + \Gamma^j_{ik}A^i \tag{3} $$

克里斯托弗符号表达式：

$$ \Gamma^\alpha_{\lambda\mu}=\frac{1}{2}g^{\alpha\nu}(g_{\mu\nu,\lambda}+g_{\nu\lambda,\mu}-g_{\lambda\mu,\nu}) \tag{4} $$

曲率张量定义：

$$ R^\rho_{\lambda\mu\nu}=-\Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\mu,\nu}+\Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\nu,\mu}-\Gamma^\sigma_{\lambda\mu}\Gamma^\rho_{\sigma\nu}+\Gamma^\sigma_{\lambda\nu}\Gamma^\rho_{\sigma\mu} \tag{5} $$

Ricci张量：

$$ R_{\mu\nu} = R_{\mu\nu\lambda}^\lambda \tag{6} $$

真空球对称解

选择球坐标 $(t, r, \theta, \phi)$ ，在球对称、静态时空条件下，度规有如下形式

$$ g_{\mu\nu} = \begin{pmatrix} -f & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & g & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & r^2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & r^2\sin^2\theta \\ \end{pmatrix} \tag{7} $$

其中 $f,g$ 只与 $r$ 有关

文献一般将分量设成未知函数的指数函数，不过计算出结果是一样的

由此求出 $g^{\mu\nu}$

$$ g^{\mu\nu} = \begin{pmatrix} -\cfrac{1}{f} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \cfrac{1}{g} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \cfrac{1}{r^2} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & \cfrac{1}{r^2\sin^2\theta} \\ \end{pmatrix} \tag{8} $$

所有非0的度规导数：

$$ \begin{gathered} g_{00,1} = -f_r \quad g_{11,1} = g_r \\ g_{22,1} = 2r \quad g_{33,1} = 2r\sin^2\theta \quad g_{33,2} = 2r^2\sin\theta \cos \theta \\\end{gathered} \tag{9} $$

因为度规有对角形式，因此联络只有三个指标至少两个相等时不为0：（下式不使用Einstein求和）

$$ \begin{gathered} \Gamma_{\alpha\alpha}^\lambda = -\cfrac{1}{2} g^{\lambda\lambda} g_{\alpha\alpha,\lambda} \quad \Gamma_{\alpha\alpha}^\alpha = \cfrac{1}{2} g^{\alpha\alpha} g_{\alpha\alpha,\alpha} \quad \Gamma_{\alpha\lambda}^\lambda = \cfrac{1}{2} g^{\lambda\lambda} g_{\lambda\lambda,\alpha} \\ \\\end{gathered} \tag{10} $$

所有非0联络：

$$ \begin{gathered} \Gamma_{00}^1 = \cfrac{f_r}{2g} \quad \Gamma_{11}^1 = \cfrac{g_r}{2g} \quad \Gamma_{10}^0 = \cfrac{f_r}{2f} \\ \Gamma_{22}^1 = -\cfrac{r}{g} \quad \Gamma_{12}^2 = \cfrac{1}{r} \\ \Gamma_{33}^1 = -\cfrac{r\sin^2\theta}{g} \quad \Gamma_{13}^3 = \cfrac{1}{r} \\ \Gamma_{33}^2 = \sin\theta \cos\theta \quad \Gamma_{23}^3 = \cot\theta \\ \\\end{gathered} \tag{11} $$

下面求Ricci张量。因为球对称，因此只求对角部分。我们只有两个关于 $r$ 的未知函数，因此只需求两个分量，我们选择 $R_{00},R_{11}$ 。为此先求曲率张量：

$$ \begin{gathered} R_{001}^1 = -\Gamma^1_{00,1} -\Gamma^1_{00}\Gamma^1_{11} +\Gamma^0_{01}\Gamma^1_{00} \\ = -(\cfrac{f_r}{2g})_r -\cfrac{f_r}{2g}\cfrac{g_r}{2g} +\cfrac{f_r}{2g}\cfrac{f_r}{2f} \\\end{gathered} \tag{12} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} R_{002}^2 =-\Gamma^1_{00}\Gamma^2_{12} \\ = -\cfrac{f_r}{2rg} \\\end{gathered} \tag{13} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} R_{003}^3 =-\Gamma^1_{00}\Gamma^3_{13} \\ =-\cfrac{f_r}{2rg} \\\end{gathered} \tag{14} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} R_{110}^0 = \Gamma^0_{10,1} -\Gamma^1_{11}\Gamma^0_{10} +\Gamma^0_{10}\Gamma^0_{01} \\ = (\cfrac{f_r}{2f})_r -\cfrac{f_r}{2f} \cfrac{g_r}{2g} +\cfrac{f_r}{2f} \cfrac{f_r}{2f} \\\end{gathered} \tag{15} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} R_{112}^2 = \Gamma^2_{12,1} -\Gamma^1_{11}\Gamma^2_{12} +\Gamma^2_{12}\Gamma^2_{21} \\ =-\cfrac{g_r}{2rg} \\\end{gathered} \tag{16} $$
$$ \begin{gathered} R_{113}^3 = \Gamma^3_{13,1} -\Gamma^1_{11}\Gamma^3_{13} +\Gamma^3_{13}\Gamma^3_{31} \\ =-\cfrac{g_r}{2rg} \\\end{gathered} \tag{17} $$

根据真空无物质爱因斯坦方程

$$ R_{\mu\nu} = 0 \tag{18} $$

我们有

$$ \begin{cases} R_{00}=R_{001}^1+R_{002}^2+R_{003}^3=0 \\ R_{11}=R_{110}^0+R_{112}^2+R_{113}^3=0 \\ \end{cases} \tag{19} $$
$$ \begin{cases} -\left(\cfrac{f_r}{2g}\right)_r -\cfrac{f_r}{2g}\cfrac{g_r}{2g} +\cfrac{f_r}{2g}\cfrac{f_r}{2f}-\cfrac{f_r}{rg} = 0 \\ \left(\cfrac{f_r}{2f}\right)_r -\cfrac{f_r}{2f} \cfrac{g_r}{2g} +\cfrac{f_r}{2f} \cfrac{f_r}{2f} -\cfrac{g_r}{rg} = 0 \end{cases} \tag{20} $$
$$ \begin{cases} -2f(f_{rr}g-f_rg_r) -ff_rg_r +gf_r^2-\cfrac{4gff_r}{r} = 0 \\ 2g(f_{rr}f-f_r^2) -ff_rg_r +gf_r^2 -\cfrac{4f^2g_r}{r} = 0 \end{cases} \tag{21} $$
$$ \begin{cases} -2fgf_{rr}+ff_rg_r +gf_r^2-\cfrac{4gff_r}{r} = 0 \\ 2fgf_{rr} -ff_rg_r -gf_r^2 -\cfrac{4f^2g_r}{r} = 0 \end{cases} \tag{22} $$

两式相加得

$$ (fg)_r = 0 \tag{23} $$

所以

$$ fg=C_0 \tag{24} $$

选一个式子代入后解得

$$ \begin{cases} f=C_2\left(1 - \cfrac{C_1}{r}\right) \\ g=\cfrac{C_3}{1 - \cfrac{C_1}{r}} \\ \end{cases} \tag{25} $$

在无穷远处，度规趋于闵可夫斯基度规；并且由爱因斯坦方程的牛顿近似 $f \approx 1-2GM/r$ ，可以确定出其中的常数。由此得到Schwarzschild度规

$$ g_{\mu\nu} = \begin{pmatrix} -\left(1-\cfrac{2GM}{r}\right) & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \cfrac{1}{1-\cfrac{2GM}{r}} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & r^2 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & r^2\sin^2\theta \\ \end{pmatrix} \tag{26} $$

或者用不变距离表示

$$ \mathrm{d}s^2 = -\left(1-\cfrac{2GM}{r}\right)\mathrm{d}t^2+\cfrac{\mathrm{d}r^2}{1-\cfrac{2GM}{r}}+r^2\mathrm{d}\theta^2+r^2\sin^2\theta\mathrm{d}\phi^2 \tag{27} $$

其中 $M$ 为黑洞质量。
